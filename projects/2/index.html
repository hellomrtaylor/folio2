Online Check In for Booking.com

UI/UX, strategy

The ask

Create the best check in experience possible across hotels, hostels and vacation rentals.

The solution

Simple online check in allowing guests to skip the front desk and go straight to their room.

The process

What makes a great check in? Of course we had our own experience, but we needed to know what others thought. Together with my Product Manager we conducted guerilla research - asking people at a cafe about their check in experiences. We took the insights from this and ran a survey to Booking.com guests across the world, to verify the results at scale.

Armed with this research we ran a design sprint where a cross functional team of designers, product managers and researchers generated, challenged and iterated on concepts. We took these concepts and some early UI to remote usability testing with guests.

When our MVP was built, we beta tested with real guests at real hotels to get the feedback we needed to perfect the experience.
