App Store for BookingSuite

UI/UX, illustration, strategy

The ask

Transform the hotel software industry by making software simple to try, integrate and purchase.

The solution

A jargon free app store with all the information you need to compare hospitality software, coupled with 1 click setup and easy billing.

The process

I kicked off the project by consuming research and knowledge from within the company. Why is hospitality software so hard to set up? How do users interact with software in their day to day? How do users find, try and even talk about software?

With this knowledge, and a list of experts to enlist, I ran a design sprint involving the key stakeholders on the project: leadership, product managers, developers, copywriters. Following a slightly adapted version of the GV design sprint process after 4 days we had a concept, high level user journey and initial features.

After some time fleshing out a usable prototype we interviewed hoteliers and had them use our prototype. I gathered feedback from participants, and wrote up the trends as a list of improvements for our product.

Come launch time, The App Store by BookingSuite was received extremely well with thousands of hotels signing up to try out software easier than ever before!
